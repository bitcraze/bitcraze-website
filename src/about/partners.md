# Advancing the Future of Robotics <span class="highlight-green">Together</span>
Bitcraze is dedicated to enabling exploration, innovation, and progress in the robotics field. Through strategic partnerships with academic institutions, research organizations, technology companies, and distribution partners, we extend the capabilities of our platform and accelerate the development of new applications and discoveries.

Our Crazyflie<sup>®</sup> ecosystem is used worldwide in cutting-edge research on swarming, navigation, autonomy, human-robot interaction, and artificial intelligence. By partnering with us, you join a global community committed to advancing the state of the art in robotics and expanding its real-world impact.

<div class="content-card" markdown="1">
## Why Partner With Bitcraze
We believe meaningful progress happens through collaboration. Our partners benefit from access to a mature and widely adopted open robotics platform, deep technical expertise, and a vibrant global user community. Together, we can explore new applications, reach new audiences, and create solutions that address complex challenges.

Partnership benefits include:
- Access to a proven, modular platform used in research and industry worldwide
- Direct collaboration with our engineering and product teams on projects and solutions
- Visibility within the global Bitcraze community of developers, researchers, and innovators
- Commercial and technical enablement resources, including training, co-marketing, and early access to new technologies
</div>

## Partnership Opportunities

<div class="arrow-headings" markdown="1">
### Academic and Research Collaboration
We collaborate with universities, research institutes, and laboratories that push the boundaries of robotics. Our platform supports a wide range of research areas, from swarm coordination to advanced perception and interaction studies. Bitcraze provides technical support, joint development opportunities, and visibility through joint projects, publications, and events.

### Commercial and Solution Partnerships
We engage with companies that are exploring new applications for indoor drones in areas such as industrial inspection, automation, creative technology, and human-machine collaboration. Partners gain access to integration support, roadmap alignment discussions, and opportunities to develop new solutions based on the Crazyflie platform.

### Distribution and Channel Partnerships
Our distribution partners play a crucial role in making Bitcraze technologies accessible to innovators and researchers worldwide. We collaborate closely with distributors who have established relationships with universities, educators, and R&D markets. Partners receive training, sales enablement materials, and ongoing support to grow their local presence and deliver value to customers.

### A Collaborative Approach
We view partnerships as long-term relationships built on trust, transparency, and shared objectives. Whether the goal is to advance academic research, develop new commercial solutions, or expand market reach, we approach each collaboration with a focus on mutual value and meaningful outcomes.
</div>
